## 🔐 About HackersBun

**HackersBun** is a real-time cybersecurity and AI news aggregator that fetches the latest security news from the world's most trusted cybersecurity publications. Stay informed about the latest breaches, vulnerabilities, attacks, and cyber threats - all in one place.

### Why HackersBun?

- **Real-time Updates** - Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds
- **Curated Sources** - Only trusted cybersecurity news outlets
- **Dark/Light Mode** - Beautiful bento-style design
- **AI News Tab** - Separate feed for AI and ML news
- **Copy & Share** - Easy to share news with your team

## ✨ Features

- 🔄 **Auto-Refresh** - Real-time news every 30 seconds
- 🌙 **Dark/Light Mode** - Toggle between themes
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Works on mobile & desktop
- 📋 **Copy News** - One-click copy title + summary + URL
- 🔗 **Open News** - Open any article directly
- 🏷️ **Category Filters** - Filter by: All, Breaches, Vulnerabilities, Attacks, Microsoft, AI Security
- 📊 **Live Stats** - Shows article count & last update time
- ⚡ **Fast Loading** - Optimized proxy system
- 🎨 **Bento Design** - Modern 3D-style UI
- 🚀 **AI Flash Ticker** - Scrolling AI news at top

## 📱 How to Use the App

### Navigation
- **All Tab** - Shows all cybersecurity news
- **Breaches Tab** - Data breach news only
- **Vulnerabilities Tab** - CVE & vulnerability updates
- **Attacks Tab** - Attack & exploit news
- **Microsoft Tab** - Microsoft security news
- **AI Security Tab** - AI-related security
- **AI News Tab** - General AI news (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)

### Features
- **Hover on any card** → Shows Open/Copy buttons
- **Click Open** → Opens article in new tab
- **Click Copy** → Copies title + summary + URL to clipboard
- **Toggle Auto** → Enables 30-second auto-refresh
- **Theme Toggle** → Switches dark/light mode
